1. Introduction

IlmAI ("we", "us", or "our") is an Islamic Q&A app that provides AI-powered answers to Islamic questions, a Quran reader, and a Hadith explorer. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

By using IlmAI, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

📋

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (hashed securely via Supabase Auth — we never see your raw password)
  • Profile information you optionally provide

2.2 Questions & Chat History

Questions you submit to the AI are sent to our backend (Supabase Edge Function) and forwarded to OpenRouter's API to generate a response. Your conversation history is stored on our Supabase database to allow access across devices. Chat data is also cached locally on your device.

2.3 App Settings & Preferences Stored locally only

The following are stored only on your device and are never sent to our servers:

  • Selected language and madhab (Islamic school of thought)
  • Text size preferences
  • Last-read position in Quran and Hadith
  • Daily question usage count
✅ Your personal preferences stay on your device. Uninstalling the app removes them entirely.

2.4 Quran Content

Quran text files (Arabic, English, Bengali) are downloaded from our Supabase Storage on first launch and saved to your device. These files are static Islamic texts and contain no personal data.

2.5 Subscription & Purchase Information

If you subscribe to IlmAI Pro, purchase transactions are processed by RevenueCat and the respective app store (Google Play). We do not store your payment card details.

2.6 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g., feature usage frequency, error reports) to improve the service. This data does not identify you personally.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Chat history: Stored until you delete conversations or your account.
  • Local data: Stored on your device; deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Purchase records: Retained by RevenueCat and the app store per their policies.
🛡️

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS / TLS.
  • Supabase enforces Row Level Security (RLS) so users can only access their own data.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

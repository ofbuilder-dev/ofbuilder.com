Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Renu ("Company", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at ofbuilder.apps@gmail.com.

When you use our mobile application (the "App"), we appreciate that you are trusting us with your information. We take your privacy very seriously. In this privacy notice, we describe our privacy policy. We seek to explain to you in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Renu is designed to be privacy-first. We do not ask for your bank login credentials, nor do we scan your emails. The data you enter into the app (such as subscription names, costs, billing cycles, and renewal dates) is stored directly on your device. We do not automatically collect personal information, usage data, or sensitive financial data to send to our servers.

If you contact us directly for support via email, we will receive your email address and the contents of your message.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

Because the app stores data locally on your device, we do not use your data for targeted advertising, nor do we sell it to third parties. Any information we receive when you contact support is used exclusively to answer your questions and resolve your issues.

3. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties for their promotional purposes. All of your subscription tracking data remains on your local device.

4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We keep your support email communications only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy unless otherwise required by law.

5. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

Your data is primarily secured by your device's own operating system security (such as your lock screen passcode or biometrics). We recommend keeping a screen lock on your device to protect your locally stored data.

6. Do We Collect Information From Minors?

We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under 18 years of age. By using the App, you represent that you are at least 18 or that you are the parent or guardian of such a minor and consent to such minor dependent’s use of the App.

7. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

Since your subscription data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete your data at any time by simply deleting the application or clearing the application's data through your device's system settings.

8. Updates To This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this privacy policy frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.

9. How Can You Contact Us About This Policy?

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may email us at ofbuilder.apps@gmail.com.
